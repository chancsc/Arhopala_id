Arhopala varro selama is a rare and small species belonging to the camdeo group and the camdeo subgroup. It is notably distinguished by its metallic silvery blue upperside and specific underside alignments.

Underside Identification Keys

Position of Hindwing Postdiscal Spot 6

Upperside Characteristics

Female: Pale sky blue.
