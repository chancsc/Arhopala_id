Arhopala arvina adalitas is a rare member of the rama group. It is a tailless species primarily distinguished by the shape of its hindwing spots and specific forewing alignments.

Underside Identification Keys

Forewing Markings:

It normally possesses a costal spot in space 10.

Structural Features: The species is tailless.

Size: Forewing length is approximately 18.0 mm.

Position and Shape of Hindwing Postdiscal Spot 6

Overlap: The spot typically touches or overlaps the end-cell bar.

Shape: The outer edge of the postdiscal spot in space 6 is outwardly sinuous or concave.
